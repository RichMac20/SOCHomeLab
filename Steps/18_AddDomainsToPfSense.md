# Add Domains to PfSense (DC1) <img width="100" height="100" alt="download" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9d495425-6756-4d5b-89c0-eff7074dda82" />
- Launch PfSense WebConfig 
  - Status 
  - DNS Resolver
  - Select 'Related Settings'
    - Scroll down to 'Host Overrides'
    - Select 'Add'
    - Provide the Names:
      - 'mail' for DC1
      - 'postifx' for Kali
    - Provide the Domains:
      - 'homelab.local' for DC1
      - 'lab.local' for Kali
    - Provide the IPs:
      - 192.168.50.101 for DC1
      - 172.16.0.10 for Kali
    - Select 'Apply Changes'
